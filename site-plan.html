Overview

Purpose

This site intends to provide a list of astronomical phenomenae happening in the area in the near future, all in a convenient way for users to plan times to see them. It could also track comets and provide their current location.

Audience

This site is intended to cater to people who like seeing astronomical phenomenae and would be willing to travel small distances to see them.
